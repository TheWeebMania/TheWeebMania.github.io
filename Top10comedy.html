Top 10 Comedy

By: Ruzchel Lloyd Estrella

A genre where multiple characters and/or events causing hilarious results. These stories are built upon funny characters, situations and events. Designed to make its audience laugh and all in all feel good and have a great time. So with that, here are the top 10 anime that has made me laugh all through my otaku life:

10. Baka To Test To Shoukanjuu

A battle system, where a student is allowed to have summoned beast and make them battle, is applied to a school and is now being used by students. A show that has never failed to make me laugh and has a charm that makes you feel at peace. It is full of funny jokes that the somehow naturally gets in with the dialogue and it will just make you gasp for air!

9. Seto No Hanayome

What happens if you suddenly get engaged to the princess of a mermaid kingdom? Well you will find out that it is quite alot of work to deal with by watching this show. It has its antics that will make you laugh and it is really a good time though the animation may be quite dated campared to later series it is really fun and the story just makes up for it. Overall this series is very good if you want to have a good time.

8. The Melancholy of Haruhi Suzumiya

HIgh school crazy antics does not stop with the series that sparked a dance craze to the whole world. God himslef became a highschool anime girl and is doing whateer she wants. Kon, a normal high school boy,  gets dragged along these antics and you just know that it will be a hilarious show were you can laugh until you are out of breath. 

7. Nichijou

Daily life encounters from what to do when a deer shows up to what happens when you go to school this show has it all. You will laugh from the gagas that are quite relatable and the cenes that just depicts it from a different perspective. The comedy on this show really is a 10/ 10.

6. Daily Lives of High School Boys

High school is an unforgettable part of our lives and these boys takes it to another level. Ranging from what to do if you are late and what to do during classes these boys just has you covered for anything. It is really funny and relatable because they do not seem to have a  serious time it really is a jokingly good and chill series that you can watch if you want to laugh at their crazy yet hilarious antics. 

5. Sakamoto DEsu Ga?

The perfect highschool boy doesn't exi- That is what this show is about the coolest guy that ever existed and he is attending high school. Many want to bring him down but as they do so they end up liking him even more. This show is full of gags about how a perfet guy would handle thins and it is the most hilarious thing ever.

4.KonoSuba: God's Blessing to This Wonderful World

You died due to an unfortunate accident and this beautiful goddess tells you the story, you find out that you can be reborn with anything that you want into a fantasy, game-like world. before you make the decision the goddess makes fun of your death because it was hilarious so you get ticked off and you decided to take the goddess with you in the adventure. This is a typical isekai that takes it to another level where the characters are mostly useless but that is what makes it fun. The whole series I was just laughing non stop each and every episode. The only downfall of this anime is that it is just too short. 

3. One Punch Man

A parody of the superhero genre which was a global hit due to its unique and comedic antics that makes fun of your typical superhero characters. Saitama is a hero just for fun and is a guy that trained himself so hard that he went bald. As a result of his training he gained super human strength and powers which is beyond him. with this he now faces a crisis of finding somone he can fight for real beacuase he now finishes his oponents with just a single punch. 

2. Saiki Kusuo no Ψ-Nan

A show that shows you what would happen to someone if they were born with psychic powers. This show just shows the struggle of a highschool boy that is struggling to have a normal life becuase if his powers which annoys him to the core what makes it worse for him is that he somehow finds himself with annoying people that just canm't seem to take a hint. It is a really lighthearted and funny show that is really a good comedy to watch that will just make you lagh with all the antics and gags that this show brings. A highly recommended show for anyone who likes comedy.

1. Gintama

To me it is the epitome of peak comedy because of its unique fuorth wall breaking and obscure references that will literally leave you gasping for air. A show that is not afraid to reference other series just to make its viewers laugh and it is damn funny when you understand it. It is a show that purely exists to make you laugh but when it gets serious, Oh damn, It gets serious. It has really quirky and loveable characters that you just won't miss. The voice acting is great and its storyline is really something that you can get hooked to. This show is the creme-of-the-crop when it some to comedy.
