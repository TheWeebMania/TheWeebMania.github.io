The Fate Guide

The Fate Franchise 

    Arguably the most difficult franchise to get into due to the complexity of its universe and the vastness of the stories that are existing within the said universe. There is no concrete timeline as to what is happening and many potential-fans are often confused by this but it really is just a matter of preference as to how you want to get intp the series each and every anime adaptation is a good entry point to the series and you just need to avoid obvious sequels then you are ready to go.

For a more In-depth answer you would need to familiarize yourself with thse terms:

1. Master - A magic user (magi) who has successfully summoned or is in command of a Servant.

2. Servant - A famous person who is summoned as a soldier in the Holy Grail War. Generally one Servant per Master, classified into one of seven classes (more, in later entries) based on fighting style, weapon choice and personal history.

3.Noble Phantasm - Are powerful armaments made using the imagination of humans as their core, and weapons or abilities owned by Heroic Spirits. They are the embodiment of the ultimate mysteries of a hero that symbolizes one's existence through historical facts and anecdotes.

4. Holy Grail War - A battle royale between magi using Servants. The last one standing can use the Holy Grail to grant any single wish.

5. Holy Grail - An extradimensional magic battery of effectively infinite power. Sometimes becomes a portal to Hell, other times used to build an airport.


    So here is how I started with the Fate franchise:

1. Fate/Stay Night

    The progenitor of the series and one of the series of where it all started. A Holy Grail War is brewing and Emiya Shirou got tangled up in participating in this war.  This is originally a visual novel with three different routes. So three of those routes are the original Fate/Stay Night route, the Unlimited Blade Works route, and finally the Heaven's Feel Route. Let me get into watching the adaptations in order:

1. Fate route- The original route with Emiya's servant, Saber, as the main lead to the story. Adapted by Studio Deen and it is often seen by fans as not necessary in understanding the universe. In my opinion, you can watch this but its storytelling is quite bad so it will be difficult to understand what is happening so going to the next route is really favorable. You should whatch this if you have time though.

2. Unlimited Blade Works- This route is adapted by a different studio and is a much better adaptation of the novel. This route's main lead is Rin Tohsaka, Shirou's schoolmate who has a very mysterious background.

3. Heaven's Feel- Adapted by the same studio as UBW, It focuses on  Sakura Matou, Emiya shirou's Junior in school who always takes care of him. Little does he know about the dark secrets hiding behing her calm smile.

2. Fate/ Zero

    The direct prequel to the events of Fate/Stay Night. Set ten years before the Stay Night series, this follows the story of Emiya Kiritsugu, The father mentioned by Shirou in the Stay Night series. What happens here is that Kiritsugu enters the Holy Grail War as part of the Einzbern Family and Has Saber as his servant.  

3. Fate/ Apocrypha

    A different timeline but from the same universe, Apocrypha features a unique Holy Grail War, instead of 7 Masters battling each other. HEre you have a Holy Grail war where 7 masters are on the same side battling another 7 masters from a different side. So in a way this is a Holy Grail War that is played like a chess where it is a piece per piece battle going on.

4. Fate/ Kaleid Liner Prisma Ilya

    A spin-off series of the Stay Night story featuring the side character Ilya Von Einzbern as the main character. Here she is a a magical girl who collects cards and brings the world at peace. It is really entertaining if you ae already hooked on the franchise because of the appearances and cameos of your loved characters from the original Stay Night Series.

5. Fate/ Grand Order

    Another series series set in parallel universe as the Stay Night franchise, This is based on a mabile game of the same name. It has One movie and a series currently ongoing which adapts the final battle or mission of the game. It is set in a world where humanity is almost wiped out from existence and in order to fix it the main character must now go back in time fix the timelines that has been destroyed by the evil organization. He must stand tall as humanity's last master.

6. Fate/ Extra Last Encore

    A story set in a different timeline from the Stay Night series, A show adapted from a PSP game. It has the same formula of a Holy Grail war set in a virtual world where the Hero finds with him an "enigmatic" saber servant.



    As of date these are the main existing animes adapted from  the Fate franchise and a lot still has not been adapted. Looking forward for more fate series and I hope this guide helps.
