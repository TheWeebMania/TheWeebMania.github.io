The Monogatari Series Guide

The Monogatari Series 

    A series that involves the entanglement of supernatural beings or apparitions with humans. Koyomi Araragi, your average and normal high school boy, crosses paths with a very dangerous vampire named Kissshot Acerola Heart Under Blade, Little does he know that this encounter will changed his life forever.

    This series is highly praised for its very intellectual storyline but at the same time confused many newcomers. Spanning from 11 seasons and counting, you have got to be over whelmed by it. So I am going to present how to best watch this series.

    There are two appropriate ways to watch this series: The Chronological and Broadcast Order.

    The best way to watch it is through the Broadcasting order because this way you will retain the mystery aspect of the story and will be introduced properly. Because by watch using the chronological order you are spoiling yourself from the fun of enjoying this series and as a fan I do not want that to happen so without further ado here is the watch order:

1. Bakemonogatari

    Contains the stories Hitagi Crab, Mayoi Snail, Suruga Money, Nadeko Snake, and Tsubasa Cat. These is the beginning of the unfolding of the mysteries where Koyomi has now met different girls who also crossed past with super natural beings and he is helping them get over it.

2. Kizumonogatari

    Even though released much later the author of the series stated that it goes best well bet ween Bake and Nise. A movie trilogy, that tells the story of Koyomi and how he became a vampire and his history with Kiss Shot Acerola Heart Under Blade.

2. Nisemonogatari

    Contains the Karen Bee and Tsukihi Phoenix stories. Where Koyomi's twin sisters now encounters apparitions that Koyomi must face in order to save his siblings.

3. Nekomonogatari: Kuro

    Tells the prequel story of the Tsubasa family. It tells how Koyomi helped his classmate Tsubasa Hanekawa during golden week. An event before bakemonogatari that explains how Hanekawa got an encounter with the sawarineko, an apparition that has the power to drain the ife force of an individual.

4. Monogatari Series: Second Season

    Somewhat confusingly named, if you have no experience with the light novels, this encompasses a big bulk of the single story light novels including the Tsubasa Tiger, Mayoi Jiangshi, Nadeko Medusa, Shinobu Time, and Hitagi End. This involves development with the girls that Koyomi hs helped up until this point.

5. Hanamonogatari

    Tells the Suruga Devil story. The story is set two years from the event of Bake, when Koyomi and Senjougahara has finally graduated and Kanbaru Suruga is on her last year in highschool.

6. Tsukimonogatari

    Tells the Yotsugi Doll story. When Araragi noticed that he no longer has his reflection in the mirror, something that vampires only posses. He worries that he is becoming a fully-fledged vampire. With this he enlists the Help of Yotsugi and her master Kagenui, an apparition expert, for help.

7. Owarimonogatari (First Season)

    Tells the Ougi Formula, Sodachi Riddle, Sodachi Lost, and Shinobu Mail stories.

8.Koyomimonogatari

    Encompasses 12 different short stories about Koyomi Araragi. A series that is much like a filler but a must watch non the less. Just short stories of what Koyomi Araragi has experienced from meeting Kiss Shot up to preparing for university exams.

9. Owarimonogatari (Season 2)

Tells the Hitagi Rendezvous, Araragi Koyomi’s story, and Ougi Dark stories as well as one other story not mentioned for spoilers sake.

10. Zoku Owari Monogatari

Released originally as a movie, but planned screenings as a 6-episode series in 2019, this tells the Koyomi Reverse story. As of now this is the latest of the anime releases but there rumors of the author continuing the series, in order to produce more and make the story progress.
