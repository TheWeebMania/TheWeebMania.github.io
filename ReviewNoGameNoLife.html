No GAme No Life

A review BY: ANdrea DE DIOs

   The series follows Sora and his younger stepsister Shiro, two hikikomori who make up the identity of Blank, an undefeated group of gamers. One day, they are challenged by the god of games to chess and are victorious. As a result, the god summons them to Disboard, a reality that revolves around games.

    If you like thinking and games then this story might be for you.No game no life literally means they live for games ;>>>. It's a story of a girl named shiro and his brother sora whose lives revolve around games, they are not social, they are mavericks and they have sibling complex . They spend most of their time playing games to the point where the became unbeatable and nothing challenges them anymore. Not Until one day, when someone claiming to be a god, challenges them to play chess. They accepted the challenged and faced difficulty in playing but manages to win at the end. After the game, the "god" asked them if they think they were born in a wrong world and ask if they want to come to his world. They said yes and they were brought to a world of their dreams, a world where you play to live, where everything is a gamble and where games decide everything. Upon arriving at the new world, they saw the current princess losing because her opponent is cheating. They tipped her that her opponent is cheating and so she asked them for help. The princess is named stephanie dola. The siblings agreed to help and was able to obtain the throne. The siblings' reign started. The story focuses on how they are bringing "the lowest race" (their kingdom) up to the highest and expand their kingdom's teritory. But their main goal is to find out what the "god" wanted them to do? What is their main purpose? This anime can be mindblowing and all in all, the anime is AMAZIIIING!!!
