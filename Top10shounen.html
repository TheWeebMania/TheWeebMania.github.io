Top 10 Shounen

By: Ruzchel lloyd Estrella

"Shounen" is a Japanese word which literally means "Young Boy". So that means that the Shounen genre is audience is of young boys. But not only young boys enjoy this genre in fact many people of different ages express their love and support for shounen anime. This is because of most of the mainstream anime belong to this genre. And with that here 10 of the best shounen anime in the market 

10. Assassination Classroom

A show that features the story of a class who was given the task of assassinating their teacher, a octopus-like supercreature, before their school year ends. It is a thrilling ride that is filled with comedy, action and very lovable characters that makes you remeber them. It is a highly recommended show and is a great series to watch anywhere.

9. Katekyo: Hitman Reborn!

Reborn! is a really cool show to watch when you have time and it is about someone who got dragged in the world of mafias by force and he does not want to do it. The show starts off slow but once it reaches a certain part you will immediately get hooked. It has its low and annoying points but it is easily saved by the badassery that comes after it. That is why this show is a must watch.

8.Yu YU Hakusho

A classic throwback series that does not fail to captivate its audience even to this day and age. Even though the animation is quite dated its storytelling and plot is something that can be consumed by viewers o all ages. What this series is best known for is its tournament arc, which is arguably the best tournament arc that an anime has given its viewers.

7. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

A really new series that just aired. It is still quite young but the story and its characters is really memorable. Some might say that this show has a really generic story for its genre but here me out on this. What sets this apart from other shows is because of its godly animation done by the studio who adapted it. It gained massive attention when its 19th episode aired and you will imediately be hooked to the series.

6. Boku no Hero Academia

Hero academia is a fairly new addition to the genre but its fame made it all around the world.It elevates your normal school setting anime. From many lovable characters that the show clearly does a great job of developing it really is remarkable. It even captured the hearts of the western viewers from fanarts and different cosplays this series is clearly a fan favorite! It takes the school setting and stereotypical events happening there to a level in which the viewers really connect to. 

5. Hunter X Hunter

This show will seem childish at first but ohh you are so wrong. THe authorship of the show is just marvelous from its fights thatvare really strategical and of not using the formula of just being really strong through the power of friendship is really amazing on its own. You will see the genius of this show through its main characters and how they take on adventures. The only downfall of the series is the manga's frequent hiatus so the story does not progeress as much as of date.

4. Full Metal Alchemist: BRotherhood

A Full Metal Alchemist adaptation that is faithful to the original story. It is about two brothers who are trying to get back their bodies after trying forbidden alchemy techniques. What makes this show great is the fact that it is branded by most fans as "The Most Complete Anime" in terms of storytelling. And I can see why because I was left really satisfied when I finished watching it. Everything got wrapped up in a way that nothing is left untangled and it is widely praised for that.

3. Naruto

"I'm gonna be the hokage!" a line that is widely used in the show by the main character. A classic series when you talk about in public. It has a storyline that makes you feel like you are part of the story. The fight scenes are just right and it is really well choreographed. This show is widely known not only in Japan but also the greater part of the of West. It is a really good show that you won't forget once you have watched it and you better "Believe it!"

2. Dragon Ball

A masterpiece that has captivated its audience from the moment it aired up, nearly three decades ago, up until the present time. It takes the most basic formula of foghting and presents it in a way that the viewers can remember. It has a reason as to why It has reached peak popularity even outside of Japan and is widely known, 

1. One Piece

A show that goes over 900 episodes and counting. It has a storyline that can make you laugh with jokes, get hyped up for awesome fights, and even make you cry for the characters that expresses their will and wants. A story about a pirate who dreams about becoming the next pirate king. He gathers his crewmates to set sail for the treasure left behind by the previous pirate king, Gol D. Roger. In this adventure they explore the vast oceans and take take on the most dangerous sea known to man, The Grand Line. By the end of that sea it is said that One piece, the greatest treasure left by the Pirate King resides. It is a really wide series that has it all and all this now needs is a great ending to wrap it all up. So you better go and watch it now
