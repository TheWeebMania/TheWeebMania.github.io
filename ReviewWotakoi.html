Wotakoi: Love is Hard for an otaku

A Review By: Andrea De Dios

    26-year-old Narumi Momose is a die-hard fujoshi otaku who recently switched jobs, in hopes of being able to play off an image of a perfect, beautiful young lady. However, her hopes are crashed when she reunites with her middle school classmate Hirotaka Nifuji, an avid gamer otaku that knows about her secret.

    Its a cute and unique story about otakus (as the title says). --- a closet otaku who is afraid of being dumped by his boyfriend if they know the truth. She transfer into a new workplace where she met her childhood game otaku friend. After experiencing break ups from her past boyfriends she admits its hard to stop ang pretend not to be an otaku. Then they realize that they can be themselves without suppressing their hobbies and decided to hit off and became a couple. They were friends with another couple, --- their boss and ---, who also share the same hobbies.

    Its a light shojou and the anime shows BL mangas which excite me more. It also taught me the terms about yaoi, the titles seme and uke, and what the fans of yaoi or BL are called fujoshis. It was my first anime that explains and feature what it is like to be a fujoshi. Some hobbies arent always accepted or can be publicly exposed (especially yaoi hihi), especially if you don't want your partner or even your friends to be turned off. But don't be afraid, maybe your significant or true friends can acceñt it although it may make them a little uncomfortable
