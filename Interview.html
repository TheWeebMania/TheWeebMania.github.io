Artist Interview

Conducted By: Ruzchel LLoyd Estrella

Up Close and Personal with the artists

       People in the anime community are creative geniuses who excel in many fields such as: cosplay, voice acting, arts & crafts, and of course drawing. Many artists have emerged from loving anime. Thus a lot of their craft is greatly inspired by the medium and masterpieces are made.

    Just like most of them young and hopeful artists such as Jenina and Lara have shared with us their thoughts as to how they got started. 

LARA:

*When asked what she liked to draw she stated:

    "Yung gusto kong i-drawing ay yung mukha syempre, as it displays the emotion of the character and dun mo mai-a-identify kagad kung anong klase ng personality kaya gusto kong nag-dodrawing ng mukha."

When Translated: "I like to draw faces ofcourse, as it displays the emotion of the character and that is where you can quickly Identify the kind of personality the character has and I love portraying them very much."

    This shows how she feels about when drawing and how she views her drawings. She likes to give them personalities  and not just a straight-faced guy and that is how she draws.

*When asked what inspired her to draw she stated:

    "Yung nag-inspire talaga sakin na magdrawing ay yung big bro ko and yung hilig namin sa anime. Bale yung dalawang yun ang nagspark talaga ng interest ko na pasukin yung mundo na ‘to which is worth it naman."

When Translated: "What really inspired me to draw is my big brother and our love for anime. So these two things are what sparked my interest in pursuing the world of art and for me it is really worth it.

    This states that she really enjoys what she does and drawing is really something that she loves. She says that it is worth it that is what clarifies this statement.


JENINA:

When asked the same questions she stated:

    "I like drawing since when I was a kid. The thing that sparked my interest in this field was my first encounter with the Japanese culture—specifically anime. Usually when I’m illustrating, I have many thoughts and inspirations that I want to do yet as much as possible, I try not to restrict myself and let my hand express whatever creativity it could bear. These days, I’m more into learning and discovering new styles that I could apply in my technique so that I could further improve myself in delivering the imagination I could put into my art."

    With the same sentiments as Lara she really likes what she does and is not afraid to show the world her passion for it.



    They are young artists who excels in their feild and is really motivated by their. How about you? what is your anime story?
