Howl's Moving Castle

A review by Andre De Dios

    Sophie  has an uneventful life at her late father's hat shop, but all that changes when she befriends wizard Howl, who lives in a magical flying castle. However, the evil Witch of Waste takes issue with their budding relationship and casts a spell on young Sophie, which ages her prematurely. Now Howl must use all his magical talents to battle the jealous hag and return Sophie to her former youth and beauty.

    Have you ever met someone that changed you or your life? howl did meet someone like that. Howl's moving castle is a great film. Although it seems and is old, the story and graphics can't be belittled. The story starts of with a girl named sophie who got involved with howl, a wizar, who's being chased by the jealous witch of watse. When the witch learned about the interaction between howl and sophie, the witch planted a curse on sophie causing her to look like an 80 year old grandma. Sophie, hoping to find a cure, search for howl's moving castle and started to work for him. She started cleaning the castle, shoping and cooking food for him and his assistant called markle. One day, a witch called suliman summoned both howl and the witch of waste but because howl is afraid to go, sophie, in behalf of howl, came as his mother. While coming to suliman's castle, the witch of waste was put into suffering (trying to remove her spell and such, she was made to walk and such)and soon regain her true self an old fat grandma. Howl soon arrived at the scene disguised as the king but was busted as the king himself greeted suliman. Suliman intimidated everyone with her magic but howl and company manages to escape. When they went back to the house, howl adjusted the house for everyone, relocating the house to sophie's village where bombing was about to happen. Sophie's mom came to visit and gave gifts which were from suliman. The witch smoked the cigarette from the gifts causing calcifer(the fire with howl's heart and the one keeping the castle together) to become weak. Howl discovers it, threw it away and soon leaves the house to fight the bombing. Then the witch tried to get howl's heart from calcifer causing her burns and making sophie throw water on calcifer. Calcifer became really really weak, to make him stronger, sophie gave him her hair. Sophie tried to relocate and made the castle fall apart. With the small part of the castle and her company, she tries to save howl. Upon travelling she fell and saw a door leading to howl's past and later saving him. The story ends with a happy ending showing howl and company travelling in their own little castle
