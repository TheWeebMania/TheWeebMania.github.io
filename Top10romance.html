Top 10 Romance

By Ruzchel Lloyd Estrella

A tale of two people falling in love. It tells the story of how the characters find and express their feeling towards their loved ones. This genre is actually one of my favorites as far as an anime watcher is concrbed. I love it because every time I watch it makes my heart go "doki-doki" for the characters and I love that. With that, here are the top 10 romance anime that I have watched:

10. Clannad: After Story

A story about how to be a Tomoya and Nagisa fell in love and eventually started their own family. This is a direct sequel to the anime Clannad and it continues on what happens after those events. This anime isreally heartwarming and amazing. It has a storyline that can really make you shed some tears and it is highly appreciated by fans of all generations. You first need to watch the first season though before you jump in this show.

9. Plastic Memories

What happens if you fall in love with someone who you know will disappear in just a shortperiod of time? In a world where androids called giftia, who resembled humans and can understand human emotions, are all over the place are used for someone who lacks the emotional suppport of loved ones. However they are not perfect, they can only retain their emotions for a certain amount of time and they must be retrieved right after this or else they might go rogue. Now Tsukasa Mizugaki cross paths with Isla, a giftia who is about to run out of memory, on his work. What will happen to these two now? It is a story of romance that you wil be sure to cry about as it is really touching.

8. The Pet Girl of Sakurasou

A story of love, friendship and hardwork. After being kicked out of the normal dorm for keeping a stray cat, Sorata Kanda moves into Sakura Dormitory. As he starts to get used to the life in the dorm, Mashiro Shiina, a world famous artist who cannot even take care of her daily life, moves in. Sorata is forced to become Mashiro's handler. Now unfolds their story as they go through their high school life. It is a really amazing story and it should be watched by people who longs for something that gives you the energy of youth and what you should experience.

7. My Little Monster
How opposites attaract a very studious girl meets a boy who does not have a care about school. Shizuku Mizutani aims to be the number one student at school but she fails to do so as Haru Yoshida, a guy that is feared by the students because of beating up someone, takes it. She taught that he is some student that is very studios, to her surprise when she interacts with him he acts like a stupid boy in front of others. The story now focuses on their story as to how they developed their feelings for each other as well as the friends that they have along the way.
6. Your Lie in April
The spring is really a sad period. Kousei Arima, a child prodigy, struggles to find his passion of playing piano again. But once he crossed path with a girl named Kaori Miyazono, a violinist of the same age, he wa forced  into situations where he must play the piano again. This is the tearjerking story of how he gained his passion back for playing the piano again and giving him a treason to continue.
5. Snow White With Red HAir

Shirayuki, the local herbalist, is known in the kingdom for having a vibrant red hair and a very beautiful face. The prince of the kingdom calls her in for he wants her to be his concubine. She refuses this and once the prince threatened her to doing it she ran away from the kingdom. While running away, she encountered the the group of Zen and he helps her. To her surprise Zen was actually the second prince of the neighboring kingdom and there she was offered a position in the capital. The story now shifts into her work as a herbalist along with the building of the relationship between Pirnce Zen and Shirayuki. A very good story of how the two builds their relationship and their struggles to provide for the kingdom.

4. Kimi ni Todoke

A story between between Serinuma Sawako,a girl being isolated in class due to her "scary look " and the Kazehaya Shouta, the most popular boy in school. He takes interest in her due to a certain encounter and is finally looking at the good side of Sawako. Now the plot unfolds as he begins exploring his feelings for the girl as well as Sawako. A heartwarming love story between the outcast and the most accepted person in school. A definite must watch.

3. Ao Haru Ride

Futaba Yoshioka, a girl who likes to act tomboyish due to her middle school experiences of being bullied for being too much of a girl, meets her long lost friend who she has feelings for, Kou Mabuchi. He transfers in class and he seems to have forgotten her. The story now begins as they star interacting with each other and as the explore their old memories and feelings for each other.

2. Kaichou wa Maid-sama

Misaki Ayuzawa is the model student and is elected as the student council president in their school. Through this she meets Takui Usui the most perfect boy in school who she just can't seem to control. The only downfall of Maisaki's character is that she is por sopo she has to work as a part-time maid in a cafe. She hides this work because of the fact that it will ruin her reputation of anyone from her school finds this out. Usui, who is hanging out the cafe accidentally saw Misaki throwing out the garbage wearing her maid uniform. The fun now begins as Misaki tries to keep Usui's mouth from telling everyone of her part time job.

1. Toradora

Taiga Aisaka likes Yusaku Kitamura, Ryuuji Takasu's best friend. Ryuuji Takasu likes Minori Kushieda, Taiga Aisaka's best friend. Ryuuji and Taiga now helps each other get the heart of their respective best friends in a RomCom story that was ahead of its time. The very first anime that made me experiece the "doki-doki" feeling that's why I gave it the top spot.
