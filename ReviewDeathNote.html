Death Note

A Review By: Andrea DE Dios

    A high school student discovers a supernatural notebook that has deadly powers. He can kill anyone he wishes simply by inscribing their name within its pages. Intoxicated with his new power, he begins to eliminate those he deems unworthy of life.

    If you like stories that needs a lot of brainpower then deathnote is for you.The story revolves around a highschool boy named light yagami who lives in a world where crimes are abundant. One day , a chance to change the world was given to him when a death god named Ryuk dropped his death note in the human realm. He doubted it at first but believed it later on. Well whou would have thought that you can kill someone the way you want them to just by writing their name and details of death in a notebook is a great power right? So light wanted to change the world and started killing criminals believing he was justice but taking someone's life is still not right, that's why a pro detective that goes by the name L tries to find out the identity of the mastermind, kira ( light yagami) .
