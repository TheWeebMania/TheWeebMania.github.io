Parasyte: The Maxim

A review By: Andrea De Dios

    One night, the parasites descended from the sky, going and taking human body as it pierce to the brain without the man or woman noticing. Eating human was their source food, shaping, bending the possessed human was their way to kill. Fortunately, Shinchi survived and prevented the parasite from going into his head. Migi, the parasite who tried to take shinchi's body, take form in shinchi right arm. Together they unravel and fight parasites as they try live as both human and parasite.

    It was a little bit philosophical, as one parasite tries to be or act human. Not knowing why they were living, for what purpose are they created and why do they differ in humans. They were like robot programmed and no emotions present in them, even though some were only killing humans for fun and survival.

    Why do they have to follow human norms or be labeled as killers for what other parasite did. It was like a normal human thinking about his purpose but in fictional form which somewhat made me think about reality of life.
